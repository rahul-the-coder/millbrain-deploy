MillBrain

AI Maintenance Assistant — Paper Manufacturing Plant

MillBrain is an autonomous industrial agent that monitors 100 machines in real time. When an equipment alarm fires, it queries a plant SQLite database for sensor readings and maintenance history, scores component failure risk using a custom confidence formula, retrieves relevant troubleshooting procedures via RAG, and delivers a structured diagnosis with a recommended action — all in seconds.
